Getting Started

When you first open Happy Sappy, you'll be greeted with the Dashboard. Here's how to get started:

  1. Open the App Tap the Happy Sappy icon on your home screen to launch the application.

  3. Add Your First Expense Tap the "Add Expense" button in the bottom navigation or the floating action button.

Add Expense

Recording your expenses is the core feature of Happy Sappy. Here's how to add a new expense:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Navigate to Add Expense Tap the "Add Expense" button in the bottom navigation bar.
  2. Enter Amount Type the expense amount in the Amount field. Only positive numbers are accepted.
  3. Select Category Choose from 8 categories: Food, Transport, Shopping, Entertainment, Bills, Health, Education, or Other. Tap the appropriate chip to select.
  4. Set Frequency Select how often this expense occurs: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, or Annually.
  5. Choose Date Tap the date field to open the date picker and select when the expense occurred.
  6. Add Note (Optional) Include additional details about the expense in the note field.
  7. Save Tap the "Add Expense" button to save your entry.

Records

The Records section is where you can view, search, filter, and manage all your logged expenses.

Features

🔍 Search

Use the search bar at the top to find expenses by:

  • Category name (e.g., "Food", "Transport")
  • Note content
  • Amount

🎚️ Filter

Tap the Filter button to narrow down expenses by:

  • Category: Show only specific categories
  • Date Range: Filter by time period
  • Frequency: Filter by expense frequency

📊 Sort

Tap the Sort button to order expenses by:

  • Date: Newest or oldest first
  • Amount: Highest or lowest first
  • Category: Alphabetically by category

✏️ Edit & Delete

For any expense in the list:

  • Edit: Tap on an expense to modify its details
  • Delete: Swipe or long-press to remove an expense (confirm deletion)

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Is my data stored locally or in the cloud?

Your expense data is stored locally on your device using Room database. This ensures your financial information remains private and secure.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings > Data Management > Export Data to export all your expenses as a CSV file that can be opened in spreadsheet applications.

What happens if I uninstall the app?

Unfortunately, uninstalling the app will delete all your local data. Make sure to export your data before uninstalling if you want to keep a backup.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! Happy Sappy works completely offline. No internet connection is required for any of its features.

Features Questions

How do I edit an expense?

Go to the Records section, find the expense you want to edit, and tap on it. You'll be able to modify all fields and save your changes.

Can I delete a single expense?

Yes, in the Records section, you can delete individual expenses. Be careful as this action cannot be undone.

How do achievements work?

Achievements are automatically unlocked when you meet specific criteria (like logging your first expense, maintaining streaks, or using all categories). You'll receive a notification when you unlock a new achievement!

What's the difference between frequency types?

Frequency helps you categorize expenses by how often they occur:

  • Daily: Expenses that happen every day (e.g., coffee)
  • Weekly: Expenses that happen once a week (e.g., groceries)
  • Monthly: Expenses that happen once a month (e.g., rent)
  • Quarterly: Expenses that happen every 3 months
  • Annually: Expenses that happen once a year (e.g., insurance)

Troubleshooting

Why aren't I receiving notifications?

Check that:

  1. Notifications are enabled in Settings > Notifications
  2. Your device's notification settings allow notifications from Happy Sappy
  3. You haven't enabled "Do Not Disturb" mode

The app is running slowly

Try these steps:

  1. Close and reopen the app
  2. Check if you have a large number of expenses (consider exporting and clearing old data)
  3. Restart your device

I accidentally deleted my data

If you previously exported your data, you can import it back. Unfortunately, there's no undo function for the "Clear All Data" action, so always export before clearing.
